Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade instantly from Settings. If you upgrade, you get full access immediately. If you downgrade, changes apply at next billing cycle.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data stays yours forever. If you cancel, you keep read-only access. Reactivate anytime to pick up where you left off.

How does the Idea Satisfaction Guarantee work?

If you're on Startup or Growing and haven't found a viable business idea after researching, contact us. We'll do personalized research for you — and if we can't find something suitable, your next month is free.

Can I start with Free and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Start with 3 free checks and 1 idea analysis. When you're ready to explore more, upgrade to Startup. All your data carries over.
